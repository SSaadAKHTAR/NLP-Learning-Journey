Education is a powerful tool that shapes individuals and societies. It provides knowledge, skills, and values that help people succeed in personal and professional life. Formal education usually begins in schools and continues through higher education institutions.

Modern education has evolved with the use of digital technologies. Online learning platforms, virtual classrooms, and educational software have made learning more accessible to students worldwide.

Education also plays a critical role in social development by promoting equality, critical thinking, and innovation. A strong education system contributes to national growth and stability.
